Attributes

src - The src attribute is used to pass in the path to the form or form and submission to be displayed.

form - Alternatively, you can pass in the form definition as an attribute on the formio directive.

submissions - If submissions have already been loaded, they can be passed in as an attribute.

perPage - The number of submissions to list per page.

In addition to forms that create submissions, lists of previous submissions can also be embedded within your application. This is useful for creating lists of items to display within your application.

<formio-submissions src="{formName}"></formio-submissions>
